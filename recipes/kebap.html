Best Turkish Kebap in the City

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground meat (beef, lamb, or a combination)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriande
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Skewers (wooden or metal)

Preparation

  1. Soak wooden skewers in water at least 30 minutes to prevent them from burning.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the ground meat, chopped onion, minced garlic, parsley, cumin, coriander, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  3. Use your hands to mix all the ingredients together until well combined.
  4. Divide the meat mixture into small portions and shape them into long, sausage-like cylinders.
  5. Thread the meat cylinders onto the skewers, pressing and shaping them gently with your hands to ensure they stick together.
  6. Preheat your grill or grill pan over medium-high heat.
  7. Place the kebabs on the grill and cook for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until they are browned and cooked through.
  8. Turn the kebabs occasionally to ensure even cooking.
  9. Once cooked, remove the kebabs from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving.
  10. Serve the kebabs hot with your choice of accompaniments such as pita bread, tzatziki sauce, hummus, grilled vegetables, or a fresh salad.
